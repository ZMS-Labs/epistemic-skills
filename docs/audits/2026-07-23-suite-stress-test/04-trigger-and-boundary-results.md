## Result scale

- **SOUND** — direct source plus deterministic/live evidence supports the stated scope.
- **CONDITIONAL** — the method is coherent, but a load-bearing runtime, independence condition, or population measurement is unavailable.
- **CONTRADICTED** — a current source claim or inherited audit claim is false.
- **UNTESTED** — neither safe source/deterministic evidence nor live observation exists.
