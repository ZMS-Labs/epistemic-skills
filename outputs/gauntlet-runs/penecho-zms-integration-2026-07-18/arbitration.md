## P1 blockers and accepted design conditions

1. **Incremental value:** randomized paired crossover against rehearsed drawing/annotation -> PNG -> ordinary multimodal chat. Across 20 representative tasks over two weeks, promotion requires either >=20 percentage-point higher useful completion or >=25% lower median completion time, no material quality regression, and voluntary reuse on at least three tasks.
2. **Narrow trust boundary:** first stage uses an already-working, explicitly named cloud/API route. No LAN-reachable CLI, new local serving, production gateway edits, or cluster ingress.
3. **Reproducible evidence:** immutable PenEcho revision/image, actual model identity, settings, browser/OS/InkFlow versions, hashed tasks, rubric, and results; clean replay within seven days with 100% immutable resolution, >=95% identical classifications, and no undocumented configuration.
4. **Option 2 provenance, open and outside authorization:** one repo/owner, digest-pinned artifacts, replica route-identity agreement, Git-to-live provenance, and <=15-minute tested rollback.

## P2 execution conditions

- Availability: median intent-to-canvas <=30 seconds, failed starts <5%, >=20 sessions before reuse interpretation.
- Device segmentation: separate iPad, iPhone, and Manta/InkFlow outcomes.
- Continuity: hash/export each valuable scored canvas and record loss separately.
- Privacy: real-canvas tracing locked off; synthetic traces deleted <=24 hours with no backups.
- Route honesty: record executor, endpoint class, actual model, and fallback status per scored request.
- Local VLM is a follow-on only after incremental value; <=2 hours connection effort, no production edits, >=90% structured validity, zero silent fallback.
- CLI is a later repository-context lane only after allowlisting, disposable identity/workdir, no ambient credentials, and zero unauthorized connection/process tests.
- Fork eligibility requires both recurring modification-only workflows and quantified value exceeding maintenance.

## Authorization

- **Allowed now:** disposable, immutable, unmodified upstream PenEcho; one already-working explicit cloud/API route; paired export-plus-chat baseline; separate iPad and Manta/InkFlow scoring; iPhone as its own role.
- **Not allowed now:** LAN-reachable CLI, new local-model serving, production LiteLLM changes, k3s ingress, durable wrapper repo, continuity claims, or a fork.
- **Required terminal outcome:** Option 0/no adoption if the paired incremental-value threshold fails.
